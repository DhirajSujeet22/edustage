Diploma in Taxation Course (DTC)
The Delhi Accounts & Finance Academy offers a comprehensive Diploma in Taxation Course (DTC), designed for graduates and post-graduate candidates aiming to build a career in taxation. This six-month course provides extensive theoretical knowledge of Income Tax and GST laws, combined with practical training and live workshops, preparing students to become proficient Tax Consultants, Tax Planners, and Advisors. The course is delivered through regular live classes and practical training sessions at the Academy's premises in Delhi.
The course structure ensures that students gain both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, making them well-prepared for careers in the taxation field. The practical training, live projects, and workshops provide real-world experience, essential for excelling in this profession.
Course Objectives:
  • To provide a deep understanding of Income Tax and GST laws.
  • To offer hands-on experience with income tax returns and GST filings.
  • To prepare students for careers as taxation experts or for starting their own consultancy firms.
Key Features:
  • In-depth study of Income Tax and GST laws.
  • Practical training on tax filing procedures.
  • Live projects and case studies for real-world experience.
  • Guidance from experienced faculty and industry professionals.
  • Flexible batch timings to accommodate working professionals.
The DTC course is divided into multiple modules, each focusing on different aspects of taxation. The curriculum covers Income Tax, GST, TDS, and other direct and indirect taxes, ensuring that students are well-equipped to handle the complexities of taxation in India.
Course Modules:
Module 1: Introduction to Income Tax
  • Understanding the basic concepts of Income Tax
  • Computation of Income under different heads
  • Filing Income Tax Returns
Module 2: Introduction to GST
  • Understanding the basic concepts of GST
  • GST Registration Process
  • Filing GST Returns
Module 3: TDS and Other Direct Taxes
  • Understanding TDS provisions and compliance
  • Computation and payment of TDS
  • Overview of other direct taxes in India
Module 4: Tax Planning and Management
  • Strategies for effective tax planning
  • Tax management for individuals and businesses
  • Ethical practices in tax planning

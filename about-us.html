About Us

Home About Us

Delhi Accounts & Finance Academy is an entity incorporated under the Indian Companies Act 2013. An initiative by a team of experienced Chartered Accountants and Company Secretaries, the academy is growing under the guidance and leadership of CA Awdhesh Kumar Singh, a dynamic professional and practicing Chartered Accountant with over 15 years of experience in accounting, audit, finance, and taxation.

Delhi Accounts & Finance Academy is one of the top institutes in the financial market, accounting, and taxation sector. We are a Private Vocational Training Institute engaged in providing job-oriented skill development courses in both offline and online live class modes, with extensive practical training and live assistance in filing various tax returns under the Direct & Indirect Tax System of the country. We specialize in providing Job Oriented Vocational Training Courses in Accounts, Taxation, and the Financial Market, emphasizing Quality Education in the financial sector.

As India's economy grows rapidly, there is an increasing demand for trained and qualified financial professionals. The Delhi Accounts & Finance Academy has understood the needs and requirements of the industry through research and has designed courses to suit the needs of businesses, corporations, and the accounting and taxation industry. We contribute to nation-building by educating graduates and undergraduates to become technically sound and successful professionals in high-demand sectors such as Accounting, Finance, and Taxation.

Our objective is to equip our students with the best financial education in the Accounting, Finance, and Taxation sectors and to provide the infrastructure to help them achieve success in their careers. We aim to inculcate in them the best of creative, analytical, and technical qualifications in finance, and we also endeavour to promote life skills and management competencies to become industry leaders.

Vision & Mission

Vision

Our vision is to be the leading institute for accountancy and taxation education, empowering professionals with the knowledge, skills, and ethical grounding to excel in the financial industry. We strive to create a dynamic learning environment that fosters innovation, critical thinking, and practical expertise, shaping future leaders who will drive progress and integrity in the accounting and taxation sectors.

Mission

  • Deliver high-quality, practical, and up-to-date education in accountancy, finance, and taxation through experienced faculty and modern teaching methods.
  • Focus on the individual learning needs of our students, offering personalized guidance and support to help them achieve their academic and career goals.
  • Equip students with the skills and confidence to navigate the complexities of the accounting and taxation professions through hands-on training and real-world case studies.
  • Instill a strong sense of ethics and responsibility in our students, emphasizing the importance of integrity and transparency in the accounting and taxation fields.
  • Promote a culture of innovation by integrating cutting-edge technology and research into our curriculum.
  • Provide comprehensive career services, including internships, job placements, and continuous professional development opportunities.

Our Trainers

CA Awdhesh Kumar Singh

Awdhesh Kumar Singh is a commerce graduate and a qualified Chartered Accountant from the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India, New Delhi. With over 15 years of experience in accountancy, audit, and taxation, he is a dynamic professional running his own Chartered Accountancy firm based in Delhi. His expertise spans various domains such as Auditing, Tax Advisory, Litigation, and consultancy in both direct and indirect taxation.

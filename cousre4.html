Certificate in Personality and Skill Development (CPSD)

Course Overview

Delhi Accounts & Finance Academy offers a certificate course in personality and skill development, designed to enhance individuals' personal and professional skills. These courses cover communication skills, leadership, confidence building, public speaking, self-motivation, and interview skills.

Key Features

  • Enhanced Communication Skills: Improved verbal and non-verbal communication abilities.
  • Increased Confidence: Techniques to build self-confidence in various situations.
  • Better Public Speaking: Overcoming the fear of public speaking and enhancing presentation skills.
  • Leadership Skills: Development of leadership qualities and team management skills.
  • Professional Growth: Improved interpersonal skills leading to better professional opportunities.
  • Grooming and Etiquette: Understanding of social and corporate etiquette.

The course is specifically designed for newcomers, aiming to help candidates manage situations and handle pressures in both the workplace and personal life. It is divided into six modules, each conducted by experts through a face-to-face Personality Development (PD) program. The course covers over 140 hours of in-person classes, including workshops.

Course Modules

Module 1: Communication Skills

Communication skills involve the ability to convey information effectively and efficiently. These skills encompass both verbal and non-verbal communication, including:

  • Verbal Communication
  • Non-verbal Communication
  • Written Communication
  • Interpersonal Communication

Effective communication skills are essential in both personal and professional settings, as they help in building relationships, resolving conflicts, and ensuring clarity of message.

Module 2: Confidence Building

Confidence building involves developing self-assurance and belief in one's abilities. Key aspects include:

  • Self-Awareness
  • Positive Thinking
  • Skill Development
  • Setting and Achieving Goals
  • Body Language

Confidence is essential for taking on new challenges, making decisions, and interacting effectively with others.

Module 3: Leadership Skills

Leadership skills refer to the ability to guide, influence, and inspire others. These skills include:

  • Vision and Goal Setting
  • Decision-Making
  • Delegation
  • Motivation
  • Conflict Resolution
  • Adaptability

Strong leadership skills are crucial for managing teams, driving projects to success, and fostering a positive work environment.

Module 4: Public Speaking

Public speaking is the act of delivering a speech or presentation to an audience. Key components include:

  • Preparation
  • Practice
  • Audience Engagement
  • Voice Modulation
  • Body Language

Module 5: Self-Motivation

Self-motivation is the ability to drive oneself to take initiative and action towards goals and tasks without external encouragement. It is a critical skill for personal and professional success, encompassing several key components:

  • Goal Setting
  • Positive Thinking
  • Persistence
  • Self-Discipline
  • Intrinsic Motivation
  • Self-Confidence

Self-motivation is crucial for personal growth, career advancement, and achieving long-term objectives, as it enables individuals to remain committed and driven, even in the face of difficulties.

Module 6: Interview Skills

Interview skills are crucial for making a positive impression during job interviews. These skills encompass:

  • Preparation
  • First Impressions
  • Effective Communication
  • Body Language
  • Answering Techniques
  • Asking Questions

Strong interview skills help candidates present themselves effectively, showcase their qualifications, and increase their chances of securing the desired position

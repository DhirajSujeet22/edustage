Certificate in Industrial Accounting & Taxation (CIAT)

Delhi Accounts & Finance Academy offers a comprehensive three-month certificate course Certificate in Industrial Accounting & Taxation (CIAT), designed for students who have completed their 12th grade. This course aims to equip students with practical and theoretical knowledge necessary for careers in accounting and taxation, with a strong focus on practical training using Tally software and live projects.

The course structured approach ensures that students not only gain theoretical knowledge but also practical skills that are directly applicable to job placements in the fields of accounting and taxation. The live projects and industrial visits provide invaluable real-world experience, preparing students to excel in their professional careers.

Course Objectives:

  • To provide a foundational understanding of industrial accounting concepts.
  • To offer practical training in GST, TDS, and payroll processes.
  • To develop proficiency in Tally software and MS Office applications.
  • To enhance skills through live industrial projects and practical training.

Key Features:

  • In-depth learning of accounting principles and their practical applications.
  • Hands-on training in GST (Goods and Services Tax) and TDS (Tax Deducted at Source).
  • Comprehensive training on Tally software for accounting purposes.
  • Instruction in basic MS Word, MS Excel, and business correspondence.
  • Engagement with live projects and industrial training for real-world experience.
  • 100% Job assistance.

Course Structure:

The CIAT course comprises six modules and three projects.

  • Over 100 hours of theoretical live classes.
  • Over 100 hours of practical training.
  • Specified modules for beginners.

Module 1: Basic Computer Course

  • Introduction to computer hardware and software.
  • Practical training on MS Word and MS Excel, focusing on their use in industrial accounting.
  • Skill development in business correspondence via email.
  • Data preservation techniques and digital record backup training.

Module 2: Accounting Concepts

  • Fundamentals of accounting entries and core accounting concepts.
  • Preparation and management of various accounting registers such as Vouchers, Cash Book, Bank Book, Journal Register, Purchase/Sale Register, Fixed Assets Register, and Stock Register.
  • Creation of Bank Reconciliation Statements, Purchase Orders (PO), Sales Invoices, and Quotations.

Module 3: Financial Statements & Payroll

  • Preparation of Trading Accounts and Profit & Loss Accounts.
  • Drafting and regulatory compliance of Balance Sheets.
  • Payroll management, including preparation of Payroll Registers and Salary Slips.

Module 4: E-Accounting with Tally

  • Introduction and practical guide to Tally ERP software.
  • Posting entries, creating, viewing, and amending ledgers in Tally.
  • Creating groups and cost centres and extracting various reports using Tally software.

Module 5: GST & Income Tax

  • Basics of GST and Income Tax.
  • GST registration process and filing of various GST returns.
  • Understanding concepts of Supply and Input Tax Credit (ITC).
  • Basics of TDS, filing TDS returns, and understanding Income Tax Returns (ITR) and their filing process.

Module 6: Live Projects

  • Practical training using live data from companies.
  • Hands-on experience with GST and TDS return filing using live data.
  • Engagement with live projects and industry visits for practical exposure.
